Radiologie Ahrensburg

Client

Radiologie Ahrensburg

Role

Brand Strategist

Year

2025

Scope

Brand Sprint (4 weeks)

The Challenge

A respected practice that needed to feel as warm as it is precise.

A respected radiology practice in northern Germany needed to modernise its brand without losing the trust and authority it had built over 15 years. Their visual identity felt clinical and cold — patients found it impersonal.

The task was clear: bridge the gap between medical expertise and human connection, creating a brand that patients could trust and feel welcomed by.

The Approach

Precision and warmth are not opposites.

We started with deep listening sessions with the founding radiologists and their team. The insight: precision and warmth are not opposites. The new brand positions the practice as technically excellent AND deeply human.

The visual identity uses clean medical confidence paired with warm, inviting tones. Every element — from typography to colour palette to photography direction — was designed to communicate both authority and approachability.

Precision in medicine. Warmth in care. The brand needed to hold both truths at once.

The Outcome

A brand system built for trust and growth.

A complete brand system including new visual identity, patient-facing communications, signage system, digital presence guidelines, and internal brand manual. The practice reported a 30% increase in patient referrals within 3 months.

The new identity unified every patient touchpoint — from the moment they visit the website to the experience of walking through the doors — creating a coherent, reassuring presence that reflects the quality of care inside.
