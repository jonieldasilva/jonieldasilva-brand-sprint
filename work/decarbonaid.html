The Challenge

An advisory firm with decades of expertise but no brand to match.

Decarbonaid is a decarbonization advisory firm with decades of experience in strategic and financial corporate management, as well as asset & investment management across numerous industries. Their team had deep expertise in helping companies decarbonise — but the brand didn't reflect the authority or breadth of what they offered.

The Approach

Authority rooted in action, not rhetoric.

The creative direction centred on one principle: credibility through clarity. While most sustainability brands lean into green cliches and aspirational language, Decarbonaid's identity needed to convey real operational expertise — the kind built over decades, not pitch decks.

The visual system uses a restrained, structured palette and precise typography to communicate strategic rigour. Every element was designed to feel like the brand of advisors who've been in boardrooms — not a startup chasing trends.

Decarbonisation is not a trend — it's an operational reality. The brand needed to feel like decades of expertise, not a marketing campaign.

The Outcome

A brand that signals decades of experience.

A complete brand identity system including logo, visual language, website, and client-facing materials. The new brand positioned Decarbonaid as trusted advisors with the authority to guide companies through complex decarbonisation strategies — from assessment to implementation.
