The Challenge

A conversational AI brand that needed to find its voice — visually.

Blablador is a Jülich, Germany-based AI startup building conversational AI tools for the European market, with a focus on trust and data privacy. Their name is playful — a nod to "blah blah," talking — but their visual identity was all over the place: mismatched icons, inconsistent colours, and a logo that looked like it was made in a free online tool.

They needed a visual system that conveyed both technical sophistication and the approachable, conversational nature of their product.

The Approach

Machine precision meets human conversation.

The visual identity plays on the tension between machine precision and human conversation. We developed a custom wordmark that blends geometric letterforms with subtle organic curves — technically crisp but never cold.

The colour system uses deep indigo as a primary, with electric violet and warm neutrals as accents. A custom illustration language of abstract speech forms and wave patterns creates visual rhythm across all touchpoints.

AI brands don't have to look like sci-fi movies. Blablador talks to people — the brand should feel like a conversation, not a command line.

The Outcome

A complete visual system — from product to brand.

A comprehensive visual identity including logo, colour system, typography guidelines, illustration library, UI component palette, website design, and social media templates. The rebrand launched alongside their Series A announcement and helped position Blablador as the leading conversational AI platform in Germany and the European market.
