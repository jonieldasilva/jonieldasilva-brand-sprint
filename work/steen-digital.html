Role

Brand Strategist

Year

Scope

Brand Sprint (6 weeks)

The Challenge

A consulting firm with sharp thinking trapped inside a forgettable brand.

Steen Digital is a Hamburg-based consulting firm specialising in operational transformation for mid-market companies. The founders — two ex-McKinsey partners — had deep expertise but their brand felt like every other consulting firm: blue, corporate, forgettable.

They needed a brand that reflected their contrarian, founder-friendly approach.

The Approach

Rigour without rigidity.

Through the Brand Sprint, we identified Steen Digital's core tension: rigour without rigidity. Unlike traditional consultants who impose frameworks, Steen Digital works from within — embedding with founders to reshape operations from the inside.

The new brand uses bold, architectural typography and a muted earth-tone palette. The visual system conveys structure and precision, but with enough warmth to feel approachable.

Strategy without empathy is just a spreadsheet. The brand needed to feel as sharp as their thinking — and as human as their method.

The Outcome

A brand that became a client acquisition tool in itself.

Complete brand overhaul including positioning statement, visual identity, website, pitch materials, and internal templates. The rebrand coincided with a pivot to focus exclusively on founder-led businesses, and the new brand became a client acquisition tool in itself.
