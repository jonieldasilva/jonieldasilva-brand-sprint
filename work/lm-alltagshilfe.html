Care Services . Brand Sprint

LM Alltagshilfe

Client

LM Alltagshilfe

Role

Brand Strategist

Year

2025

Scope

Brand Sprint (5 weeks)

The Challenge

A care company built on compassion — but invisible in a crowded market.

A home care company in Hamburg providing daily assistance to elderly and mobility-impaired clients. The founder, Lisa Mueller, had built the business on genuine compassion — but the brand looked generic, indistinguishable from dozens of other care services in the region.

The Approach

Preserving independence, not replacing it.

The Brand Sprint uncovered a powerful differentiator: LM Alltagshilfe doesn't just provide care — they preserve independence. Every service is designed to help clients maintain their daily routines, not replace them.

The new brand narrative centres on dignity, autonomy, and the quiet heroism of everyday life. Visual identity uses warm, human photography and a colour palette that feels like home — not a hospital.

Care isn't about doing things for people. It's about making sure they can still do things for themselves.

The Outcome

A brand that feels like home.

A warm, distinctive brand that immediately set LM Alltagshilfe apart in a crowded market. New website, print materials, staff uniforms, and vehicle branding — all speaking the same language of dignified independence.
